English Lavender
Eau De Toilette For Women

Yardley's English Daisy is an elegant dewy fresh, green floral fragrance, reminiscent of flower filled meadows in spring sunshine. It opens with a burst of fresh green leaves and apple, combined with a heart of hyacinth and white rose, enhanced with warm base notes of sandalwood and musk.

Fragrance Inspiration / Story

The most cherished flower in springtime, this little perennial adds a cheerful burst of colour to meadows across England’s rambling countryside. Yardley’s cherished floral fruity fragrance brings to life the wonder of a wistful spring day through it’s warm, elegant and expressive touch.
